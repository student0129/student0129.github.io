Your privacy comes first.

Your financial profile is stored on your device. We do not upload your personal data to servers. Clarity is built with a local-first data approach, meaning the core features work offline, and your sensitive information stays with you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are the scores calculated?

Risk considers emergency fund coverage, employment status, dependents, and DTI. Opportunity considers savings rate, DTI, retirement savings relative to income, and employer match utilization. The calculations are designed to give you a directional sense of your financial health, not a precise financial statement.

Can I use the app in my language?

Yes. Clarity is designed for a global audience. UI strings, numbers, and dates are localized. The app also supports right-to-left (RTL) layouts for languages that require it.

Does it work offline?

Yes, the core features of the app work without an internet connection. Your data is stored locally on your device, so you can access your scores and insights anytime, anywhere.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. As stated in our privacy section, your data is stored locally on your device per the current implementation. We don't have access to your personal financial information.
