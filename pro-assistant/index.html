Welcome to the PRO Analysis Assistant

Make better, faster, and more confident decisions.

This tool guides you through the Five-Step 10-50-90 Value Range Assessment, a core component of the Problem, Risk, and Opportunity (PRO) Enterprise Management framework.

The goal is to create a robust, defensible range of outcomes for any decision. Instead of relying on a single-point forecast, you will develop a richer understanding of the potential futures by defining pessimistic, most likely, and optimistic scenarios.
