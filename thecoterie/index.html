Our Ethos

The Coterie is defined not just by who is in the room, but by the principles that govern our interactions.

Intellectual Humility

We believe the most accomplished leaders are the most curious. Members come to learn from one another, not to posture. The difficult questions are more valuable than the easy answers.

Confidentiality as Default

Trust is our foundational asset. All discussions operate under a strict Chatham House Rule, creating a secure space for genuine vulnerability and candid exchange on sensitive topics.

Bias Toward Action

Our conversations are not academic exercises. The goal is to equip members with diverse perspectives and actionable frameworks that can be applied to drive real-world organizational change.

A Day With The Coterie

The Coterie convenes quarterly for an intensive, full-day session. This is not a conference. It is a carefully orchestrated experience designed for maximum strategic value and connection.

MORNING SESSION

Chatham House Dialogues

Members engage in off-the-record discussions on their most pressing challenges. Topics are member-driven, focusing on governance, strategy, and leadership.

AFTERNOON INTENSIVE

Strategic Accelerators

Deep-dive workshops on complex, forward-looking topics: responsible AI, societal impact, unintended consequences, and the evolving regulatory landscape.

GUEST PERSPECTIVE

The Outside-In

A private briefing from a leading academic, policymaker, or researcher whose work directly impacts your strategic horizon—offering insights you won't find anywhere else.

FUTURE-STATE SCENARIO

Red Team Challenge

Members collaborate to dissect and "red team" a plausible but difficult future scenario (e.g., a systemic AI failure), developing response frameworks under pressure.

Membership Criteria

The value of The Coterie lies in the quality and commitment of its members. Our community is built on a foundation of shared experience, trust, and a dedication to collective advancement.

Proven Leadership & Strategic Responsibility

Members are C-level, VP, or Director-level executives with direct ownership of data, analytics, or AI strategy and its impact on business outcomes.

Commitment to Candor & Contribution

The expectation is active participation. Members come prepared to share their experiences—successes and failures—to contribute to the group's collective knowledge.

Dedication to Presence

To foster deep, meaningful connections, members commit to attending the quarterly, in-person sessions held in the Washington D.C. Metro Area.

A High-Signal Community

We deliberately grow at a measured pace to maintain the quality and intimacy of the discussions. Every member is vetted to ensure alignment with our core ethos.
