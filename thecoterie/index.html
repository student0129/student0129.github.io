Our Ethos

The Coterie is defined not just by who is in the room, but by the principles that govern our interactions.

Intellectual Humility

The most accomplished members are the most curious. Those seeking perspectives that challenge their assumptions contribute more than those proclaiming their certainties.

Urgent Patience

Both timescales matter here - the leader racing to solve today's crisis and the one contemplating decade-long implications each hold essential truths.

Pragmatic Imagination

Visionary provocations and operational constraints need each other. The tension between what's possible and what's practical is where breakthrough insights emerge.

Sacred Confidence

What's shared here, stays here. Members can voice doubts they can't share with their boards, admit failures they can't discuss publicly, and explore ideas too nascent for their teams.

Generous Exchange

Everyone here has solved problems others are facing. The expectation is contribution as much as consumption - teaching as much as learning.

Beyond the Coterie

The conversations here matter only if they change what happens outside. Every insight gained should somehow serve those who will never be invited to spaces like this.

Core, Craft, & Cause

The Coterie gathers quarterly in the Washington D.C. area for immersive sessions where data, analytics, and AI leaders evolve across three essential dimensions.

CORE

Who you're becoming as a leader.

Share what's reshaping your judgment, testing your assumptions, expanding your capacity. Learn from peers who are also discovering that leadership at this level demands constant personal evolution.

CRAFT

The frontiers of what's possible.

Explore emerging approaches, breakthrough methodologies, and technical paths no one's walked yet. Challenge and refine your thinking with practitioners who understand both the science and the art of making it real.

CAUSE

The world we're creating with every model we deploy.

Examine ripple effects, unintended consequences, and the long arc of our decisions. Build with peers who refuse to optimize for the narrow when we could architect for the whole.

Claim Your Vantage Point

From the right elevation, the path becomes clear. The Coterie offers senior data and AI leaders the perspective that comes from genuine peer exchange—where Core strengthens core, Craft sharpens craft, and Cause amplifies cause. Each member is nominated and vetted, selected for what they bring to the room and what they seek to build beyond it.

Senior Data & AI Leadership

Members are C-level, VP, or Director-level executives actively leading data, analytics, or AI initiatives with budget authority and strategic influence.

Commitment to Candor & Contribution

The expectation is active participation. Members come prepared to share their experiences—successes and failures—to contribute to the group's collective knowledge.

Quarterly Commitment

Members commit to quarterly, in-person sessions in the Washington D.C. Metro Area. This geographic concentration enables deep relationships and real-time collaboration impossible in virtual formats.

A Vetted Community

We deliberately limit membership to maintain quality and intimacy. Each nomination undergoes careful review for expertise, influence, and alignment with our ethos. This isn't open enrollment—it's a curated group where every member is chosen for the value they bring and receive.
